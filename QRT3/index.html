What is QuaRunTime 3.0?


QuaRunTime was the first event organized by the Computer Society Chapter of IEEE AJCE SB which was formed last on April 2020. The 6-day event had an active participation from over 250+ participants. Last year we had the second version of our flagship event, QuaRunTime 2.0 with 6 different events. The event had an active participation from over 300+ participants. QuaRunTime 2.0 won The Darrel Chong Student Activity Award 2022 - Bronze.

This year we are back with the third version of our flagship event, QuaRunTime 3.0 with 6 different which will run from 27th October to 31st October 2022. The top 20 finalists will be selected for the final round which will be held at Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, Kanjirapally.

Registration closes by 26th October 2022
