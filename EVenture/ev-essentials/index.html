workshop agenda

  • Calling all higher secondary school students from nearby schools! Join us for an enlightening one-day workshop on electric vehicles, held as part of the EV Conclave. Designed exclusively for high school students, the workshop will cover a diverse range of topics related to EV technology. Engaging interactive sessions and hands-on activities will give you a unique opportunity to explore electric vehicles in depth, and understand their role in shaping a sustainable future for our environment and society. Don't miss this exciting chance to expand your knowledge and fuel your passion for the future of mobility!
  • Together, we will embark on an electrifying journey, empowering students to envision a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable future through the power of electric vehicles. Don't miss this extraordinary opportunity to expand your knowledge and ignite your passion for eco-friendly innovations!
