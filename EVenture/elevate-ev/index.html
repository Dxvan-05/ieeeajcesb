Elevate EV

Basic Workshop for School Students

DAY 1
Time: 11.00 am - 12.30 pm & 01.30 pm - 03.30 pm

Speaker: Mr. Abhinav R

1.Workshop Agenda:
  • Introduction to Electric Vehicle (EV) Technology and Its Fundamentals
  • Understanding How Electric Vehicles Work: Components and Systems
  • Exploring Different Types of Electric Vehicles: BEVs, PHEVs, and HEVs
  • Environmental Benefits of EVs: Sustainability and Reduced Emissions
  • Future of EV Technology and Innovations
2.Workshop Features:
  • Tailored for High School Students to Make the Content Accessible and Engaging
  • Opportunity for Students to Ask Questions and Clarify Doubts
  • Emphasis on Environmental Impact and Sustainable Transportation Solutions
  • Q&A sessions to address participants' specific queries and concerns.
