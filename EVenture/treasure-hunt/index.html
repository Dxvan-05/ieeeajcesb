1. Treasure Hunt

TREASURE HUNT

Day 1

04.00 pm - 05.00 pm

A treasure hunt is a fun and interactive activity that can be enjoyed by individuals. The treasure hunt planned is a QR code-based hunt, participants are given a series of clues they must solve to progress to the next location or clue. Participants use their smartphones to scan QR codes that lead them to the next clue or location. It can be a great way to engage people in a fun and challenging activity while promoting problem-solving skills.
