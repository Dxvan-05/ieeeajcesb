TURBOCHARGE

Workshop on Accelerating EV Progress

DAY 1

TIME: 01:30 PM - 03:00 PM

SPEAKER:MR. PRADEEP KUMAR KELOTH

workshop agenda

  • In this dynamic workshop, we will delve into the world of electric vehicles and explore how effective collaboration can drive research and development (R&D) in the EV sector. We will showcase successful instances of technology commercialization stemming from academic research, illuminating the potential of academic-industry partnerships. Through engaging sessions, we will shed light on the instrumental roles of incubators, technology transfer offices, and industry collaborations in bringing EV innovations to the market. Participants will gain insights into the significance of industry-academia collaboration in nurturing a skilled EV workforce, paving the way for future leaders in the electric mobility industry.
  • Our workshop aims to foster knowledge exchange and bridge the gap between academia and industry. Participants will engage in lively discussions, exploring educational programs, internships, and joint initiatives that facilitate seamless collaboration between these two realms. Through captivating case studies and success stories, we will exemplify how collaborative efforts have revolutionized the EV sector, driving advancements and inspiring future innovation. This workshop presents a unique opportunity for attendees to comprehend the transformative power of working together, unlocking the potential for sustainable transportation solutions in the ever-evolving world of electric vehicles.

workshop features

  • Engage in a transformative workshop that illuminates the power of collaboration in driving EV R&D. Discover successful instances of technology commercialization from academic research, inspiring innovation in the EV sector. Uncover the pivotal role of incubators, technology transfer offices, and industry partnerships in commercializing groundbreaking EV innovations. Gain a profound understanding of the importance of industry-academia collaboration in cultivating a skilled and visionary EV workforce. Explore impactful educational programs, internships, and joint initiatives that bridge the gap between academia and industry, shaping the future of electric mobility.
